About Us

Welcome to W1 Services, a trusted destination for affordable furniture and domestic appliances in London. With years of experience in the home furnishing industry, we are proud to offer a wide selection of high-quality products designed to make furnishing and equipping your home both affordable and hassle-free.

W1 Services is the trading name of Nuxway Limited, a UK-registered company operating under company number 08888846. As a legally established and transparent business, we are committed to providing reliable products and services to our customers, maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and trustworthiness.

At W1 Services, we understand the importance of comfort and practicality when choosing furniture or appliances for your home. Our extensive collection includes electric cookers, fridges, washing machines, wardrobes, beds, mattresses, sofas, tables, and chairs, catering to diverse styles and needs. Whether you’re moving into a new property, upgrading your living space, or replacing essential appliances, we have everything you need under one roof.

Our philosophy is to combine affordability and quality, ensuring that every customer can access well-designed furniture and durable home appliances without exceeding their budget. From cost-effective wardrobes and headboards to orthopedic mattresses and premium sofas, every item is carefully selected to deliver the best value for money.

Beyond our products, our customer-focused service sets us apart. We believe in making shopping seamless and stress-free, offering fast and reliable delivery options to get your furniture and appliances to your home promptly. Our professional team handles every order with care, ensuring that your items arrive in perfect condition and ready to use.

Over the years, we have built a reputation as a dependable retailer for affordable home solutions in London. We understand that each home has unique requirements, which is why our experienced team is always ready to help customers select the right products that match their lifestyle and space. From helping young families furnish their first homes to supporting landlords and property developers with bulk orders, we serve a wide range of customers with the same dedication.

Our long-term vision is to continue growing as a reliable and customer-oriented furniture and appliance provider. We are constantly looking for ways to expand our product range, enhance our delivery services, and improve the shopping experience, both in-store and online.

At W1 Services, we don’t just sell products—we help you create a comfortable, stylish, and functional home. Our passion lies in transforming living spaces into places where comfort meets practicality, allowing you to focus on what truly matters—making lasting memories with your loved ones.
