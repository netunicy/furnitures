About Us

Welcome to W1 Services, a trusted and reliable service provider in North London with a strong reputation for professionalism, quality, and customer care. For over four years, we have proudly partnered with DHL as an official pick-up point, offering convenient and efficient parcel handling services to the local community.

At W1 Services, we believe in delivering more than just services – we deliver trust, reliability, and tailored solutions to meet the needs of every customer. Our mission is to simplify your everyday life by providing a comprehensive range of home and building services under one roof.

From house removals and end-of-tenancy cleaning to painting, plastering, and furniture supply, we strive to make every project smooth and stress-free. Our selection of budget-friendly and medium-range furniture – including dining sets, sofas, beds, and mattresses – is designed to suit every home while ensuring comfort and style.

In addition to home solutions, we specialize in building services, offering professional expertise in extensions, loft conversions, renovations, electrical and plumbing works, and more. We also provide emergency building services, ensuring quick response times for plumbing, electrical certifications, rubbish removal, fire safety compliance, and more.

Our dedicated team is committed to maintaining high-quality standards while delivering cost-effective solutions. With years of experience and a passion for excellence, we have become the go-to choice for homeowners, tenants, and businesses alike.

Choose W1 Services for reliability, efficiency, and exceptional service. Whether you need assistance with home improvements, moving, or urgent repairs, we are here to help you every step of the way.
